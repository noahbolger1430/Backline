### **A Note on Data Structure (The Tricky Part)**

The hardest part of this application is the **Availability Logic**. Do not store availability as simple text strings.

**Recommended approach:**
Store availability as "Time Blocks."
$$\text{UserAvailability} = \{ \text{start\_datetime}, \text{end\_datetime}, \text{is\_recurring} \}$$

To find the band's availability, you must perform an intersection query:
$$\text{BandFree} = \text{User}_A \cap \text{User}_B \cap \text{User}_C$$

I suggest writing a dedicated Python utility class called `AvailabilityManager` that handles this math so your views remain clean.
